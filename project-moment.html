Moment

.NET 9 SignalR Ephemeral Chat Privacy-First

A privacy-first messaging platform where rooms expire on a timer or group vote. SignalR keeps conversations instant while in-memory storage, rate limiting, and democratic shutdown flows guarantee every discussion disappears without a trace.

View Repository

Key Features

Ephemeral by Design

Every room is temporary. Whether triggered by a pre-set timer or a democratic vote by participants, once a room is closed, all data is wiped from memory. No logs, no history, no trace.

Real-Time Communication

Built with SignalR on .NET 9, Moment delivers instant message delivery and presence updates. The architecture ensures low latency even as users join and leave rapidly.

Democratic Control

Users have agency over their privacy. The "Democratic Shutdown" feature allows participants to vote to close the room immediately if the conversation veers off course.

Security & Privacy

With in-memory storage, data never touches a persistent disk. Rate limiting protects against abuse, ensuring the platform remains a safe space for genuine ephemeral conversations.
